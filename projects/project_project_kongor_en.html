Project Kongor

Project Kongor is a private server for the game Heroes of Newerth, with ~760000 registered accounts and ~10000 players online in peak hours.

Heroes of Newerth is a MOBA game based on one of the most popular maps for Warcraft III - Defense of the Ancients, at one time the game's biggest rival League of Legends. Developed in 2010, the servers were shut down in 2022.

The goal of Project Kongor was to create further opportunities to play this game, but over time it began to introduce additional content such as new features, items and balance changes.

What Project Kongor offers

  • Servers spread around the world, hosted by volunteers
  • Functioning player matchmaking system
  • Personalized profile icons
  • Watch recorded gameplay
  • Gain gold and silver for playing
  • Choose a role during character selection
  • New skins and items
