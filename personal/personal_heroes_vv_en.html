Heroes of Might and Magic V.V Heroes Pack

Heroes of Might and Magic V is a turn-based strategy game in which you build cities and recruit heroes to lead your army, gather resources and artifacts on the map, and ultimately defeat all your opponents. Heroes of Might and Magic V.V is an unofficial expansion that adds new adventure map objects, an improved random map generator, new artifacts, a revamped class system, an expanded town portal, town management, and town conversion.

What this mod offers

  • 69 new heroes

Technical detail

  • Hero data is stored in the XDB files.
  • New specializations are written in LUA.
  • Hero portraits are AI-generated, using a model I trained for that purpose.
