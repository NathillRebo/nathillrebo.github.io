Heroes of Newerth Custom Pack

Heroes of Newerth is a MOBA game based on one of the most popular maps for Warcraft III - Defense of the Ancients, at one time the game's biggest rival League of Legends. It was created in 2010, the servers were shut down in 2022.

What this mod offers

  • 15 bots
  • 47 heroes
  • 170 items
  • Talent system similar to this from Dota 2 and talents for 4 heroes
  • Completed list of Staff effects
  • Shard of the Master, an item based on Aghanim's Shard from Dota 2, and shard effects to all heroes

Technical details

  • Heroes and items data is stored in *.entity files, which are XML files mapped to C++
  • Talent system uses HTML (for UI) and LUA (for scripts)
  • Bots are written in LUA
  • New heroes use in-game models and voice lines, the special effects are written in XML
  • Icons come from various other games
