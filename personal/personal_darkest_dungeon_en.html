Darkest Dungeon Trinkets Pack

Darkest Dungeon is a tactical turn-based roguelike game in which the player explores dungeons with a team of adventurers. Its high difficulty level, unforgiving gameplay, and dark atmosphere have made the game a huge success.

What this mod offers

  • 346 new trinkets for 18 basic classes and 9 classes from other mods

Technical details

  • The items are entries in a JSON table.
  • The trinket icons are either taken from the Darkest Dungeon 2 game or are basic edits with recoloring of in-game trinket icons.
