Griftlands Content Pack

Griftlands is a roguelike deckbuilder, based on Slay the Spire, but with more RPG elements. We choose a character, complete quests, and engage in verbal or physical combat (which affects the deck used), ending the day with a boss fight. In Griftlands, we control one of three characters, each with their own unique mechanics, grafts, and storyline.

What this mod offers

  • 5 cards
  • 12 bosses
  • 40 grafts
  • 34 talents
  • 2 random events

Technical details

  • Cards, bosses, grafts, talents and random events are written in LUA.
  • Card images were made by combining gradient, simple effects and stills from promotional materials, so they don't stand out from the original images.
  • Graft icons were made by cutting and pasting parts of existing graft icons and combining them into new ones.
  • Ikony talentów zostały wykonane poprzez użycie ikon statusów bądź grafik z kart i przekolorowanie ich w taki sposób, aby były w dwóch odcieniach zieleni, aby pasowały do reszty ikon.
  • Talent icons were made by rescaling status effect icons and changing their color scheme to 2 shades of green, so they fir the original icons.
