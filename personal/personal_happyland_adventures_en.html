Happyland Adventures Map Pack

Happyland Adventures is a simple platform game, in which we need to bring a set number of happylanders to the safety zone to unlock the level exit. Bringing all happylanders to safety turns enemies into coins, and collecting all cois unlocks a bonus room.

What does the map pack offer

  • 15 levels
  • 4 bonus levels

Technical details
  • All levels and bonus levels are made with the Map Editor, which comes alongside the main game.
